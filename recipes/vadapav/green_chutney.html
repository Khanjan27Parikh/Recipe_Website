Recipe to make Green Chutney


Ingredients for Green chutney


  • 1 cup finely chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon groundnuts
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame seeds (til)
  • 2 teaspoons shredded fresh coconut
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 green chilli, seeded and chopped
  • 1/4 inch ginger, roughly chopped
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • salt
  • 1½ tablespoons water

Steps to make Green chutney

  1. Add cumin seeds, groundnuts, sesame seeds and coconut in a small chutney jar of mixer-grinder.
  2. Grind them in a grinder until smooth powdery paste.
  3. Add coriander leaves, garlic, green chilli, ginger, sugar, salt, lemon juice and 1½ tablespoons water.
  4. Grind again and make a smooth paste. Taste for salt and lemon juice, add more if required and mix well.
  5. Transfer it to serving bowl. Serve it as a condiment with snacks like rava dhokla, etc.
